# Getway-rides

Getway rides is a service that provides transport for Babcock University students who wish to go from the school to the Gateway Agro-Cargo Airport, Ilishan, Ogun State, Nigeria and vice versa. It is a web application that allows students to book rides in advance and get picked up at their desired location.
It also accounts for students who may decide to travel within the afore mentioned locations alone or with friends, or some other people they might have never met before.

## Features

- User Authentication: Students can create accounts and log in to the platform.
- Ride Booking: Students can book rides in advance and get picked up at their desired location.
- Ride Sharing: Students can share rides with friends or strangers.
- Ride Notifications: Students will receive notifications about their rides from their allocated driver.
- Ride Reminders: Students will receive reminders about their rides.
- Ride Alerts: Students will receive alerts about their rides.
- Ride Support: Students can contact support for assistance with their rides.
- Ride Feedback: Students can provide feedback on their rides.
- Ride Sharing: Students can share rides with friends or strangers.
